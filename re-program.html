mimeo programming


Data from the features recorded my the mimeograph are collected in the mimeo cloud, a kind of material library for mimeos. Material programmers use their backgrounds in material science in order to determine how the features recorded in the mimeo cloud naturally behave. From there, they re-code the material features in the mimeo cloud and project how they can be stretched for future use.
